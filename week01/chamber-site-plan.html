Site Plan for the South African Chamber of Commerce

Site Name


The chosen site name is "South African Chamber of Commerce". This name was selected to clearly represent the geographical area of South African and the primary function of the site as a hub for local commerce activities.



Site Purpose


The purpose of the South African Chamber of Commerce website is to act as a central resource for local businesses to find information on networking events, economic data, and membership benefits. It also serves to promote the South African area to potential investors and new businesses.



Scenarios

  • As a local business owner, I want to find networking events where I can meet potential clients and partners.
  • As a new resident, I'm interested in learning about the local business environment and how it can support my startup idea.
  • As a tourist, I want to know about the unique businesses and services I can find in South African.


Color Schema

The primary color for the website will be a deep blue (#005a9c), representing trust and stability. The secondary color will be a vibrant yellow (#f2a900), to inject energy and optimism into the design.



Typography

The primary font for the website will be 'Open Sans', used for both headings and body text for its readability and modern appearance.
